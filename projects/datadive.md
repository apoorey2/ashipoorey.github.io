About Data Dive: Data Dive is a semester-long project competition where members of iDSC will learn and apply Data Science towards their field of interest. Teams of 5-6 people will come together to develop a project on data analytics and principles of Data Science, and present their findings in a showcase at the end of the semester.

GitHub Repository: [apoorey2/data-dive](https://github.com/apoorey2/data-dive)
